Scientific Advisory Board

About the Board

Ants are grouped into species which are organised into genera. Seems pretty simple, no? Well, taxonomy is actually a complicated and ever-changing area. The Scientific Advisory Board exists to help us keep up with these changes in classification. Whenever a big change occurs, they will help us determine which flights must be corrected.

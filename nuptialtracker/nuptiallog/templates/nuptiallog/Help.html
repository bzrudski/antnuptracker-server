Help and Support

Users and Roles

A user account is not required to use AntNupTracker. However, to report ant nuptial flights and leave comments on flight records, a user account is needed.
There are two types of AntNupTracker users:
  • Citizen Scientist: This option is for users who are ant enthusiasts, but do not have formal training in the study of ants and do not work in a field directly related to ants.
  • Professional Myrmecologist: This option is for users who have a degree related to myrmecology, the study of ants, or who work in a field directly related to ants. These users are identified in AntNupTracker by the ant icon. In addition to the features that Citizen Scientists can perform, Professional Myrmecologists can mark flight records as "Validated" by pressing on the ant icon in the top bar of the flight detail screen. Validated flights and flights that have been recorded by Professional Myrmecologists have a green ant icon beside them in the flight list.

Help and Support

App Features

Our app has a number of features:
  • Report ant nuptial flights: Using the form in the app, users can easily record nuptial flights. Simply enter the genus and species, select the date, time and location of the flight, and optionally upload images.
  • Browse nuptial flight records: See flights that other users have reported and comment to help with species identification. Sort the list based on distance and filter based on taxonomy, date of flight, presence of images, and more.
  • Get notified: Customise your notification settings to know when flights are happening for your favourite genera and species.

Users and Roles

A user account is required to use AntNupTracker. However, it is easy and free to create an account. There are two types of AntNupTracker users:
  • Citizen Scientist: This option is for users who are ant enthusiasts, but do not have formal training in the study of ants and do not work in a field directly related to ants.
  • Professional Myrmecologist: This option is for users who have a degree related to myrmecology, the study of ants, or who work in a field directly related to ants. These users are identified in AntNupTracker by the ant icon. In addition to the features that Citizen Scientists can access, Professional Myrmecologists can mark flight records as "Verified" by pressing on the checkmark icon in the top bar of the flight detail screen. Verified flights and flights that have been recorded by Professional Myrmecologists have a green checkmark beside them in the flight list.
